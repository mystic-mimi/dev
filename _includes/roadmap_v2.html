Roadmap

Find out what is beyond the horizon for Studio-NFT.

Phase I - Join the Community

Mimi transports to the Solana network in early 2022. Join our Discord server to be a part of the community and meet other anime and NFT enthusiasts!

Phase II - Launch Offering

Every NFT holder will receive a free background. They can merge these with Mimi to give her a home on the Metaverse.

Phase III - Voting Rights

All NFT holders will have voting rights on who Mimi meets in the Metaverse. Will it be a friend, enemy, lover or magical creature? They will be launched on the Solana network.

We will build an anime series on the blockchain through voting rights, fan art and professional artists. We will have this series professionally published and sent to the holder of every New World Explorer NFT.

Phase IV - Hangouts

We will acquire land on the Solana Metaverse to bring together the community, create a space for anime artistic expression, hangouts, and New World Explorers business meetings. We will enable people to take the avatar of the NFT they own.
