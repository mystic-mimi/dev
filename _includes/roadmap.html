Roadmap

Find out what is beyond the horizon for the Mystic Mimi Collection.

PHASE I - MIMI LANDS IN THE METAVERSE!

Mimi transports to the Solana network. She has transported from one of her many scenes in the original anime series, so finds herself wearing the associated outfit.

PHASE II - “THE TEMPLE”

Mimi will firstly visit “The Temple” to be assigned a CLAN.

Every NFT holder will be airdropped a token which allows them to trade in their original NFT for a CLAN revealed NFT. This will have improved artwork, CLAN insignia and relevant colours. A community for each CLAN will be launched for CLAN business.

PHASE III - “THE DOJO”

This is where Mimi will learn her CRAFT.

Each NFT holder will again be able to merge their NFT for incorporate CRAFT accessories. After this, we will airdrop resources specific to each CRAFT. These can be traded on secondary markets, but make sure your CLAN is well equipped for “The War of the Clans”.

PHASE IV - “THE SWIRLING POOLS”

At “The Swirling Pools” stories are told and legends are made. Should Mimi become a village elder, she will gain more influence in her CLAN. However, she has to use this wisely or risk revolt.

Holders will be assigned a CASTE, this is the final evolution of artwork and attributes.

PHASE V - THE WAR OF THE CLANS

A play-to-earn game will be created to allow Mimi holders to battle or trade between CLANS, evolve their CRAFT and re-elect leaders to increase their CASTE. The Mimi Metaverse will include its own currency, which can be earned, looted or wagered in the metaverse and finally sold on the secondary market for profit.

PHASE VI - HANGOUTS AND STAKING

We will acquire land on the Solana Metaverse to bring together the community, create a space for duels, trading and wagering, hangouts, and CLAN business meetings.

We are currently looking into developing staking pools where holders can stake their Mimi NFT for a lock period, in return for the Mimi Metaverse currency.
